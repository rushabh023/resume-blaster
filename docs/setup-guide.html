Before you start

  • You need a Gmail account (e.g. you@gmail.com)
  • This is not your normal Gmail password — it's a special 16-character App Password
  • You must turn on 2-Step Verification first (Google requirement)
  • Free forever — no paid services
1

Open Google Account Security

Go to myaccount.google.com/security and sign in with your Gmail.

2

Turn ON 2-Step Verification

Under "How you sign in to Google", click 2-Step Verification and follow the steps (phone or authenticator app). You only do this once.

3

Find App Passwords

Go back to Security. In the search box at the top, type App passwords. Or find it under 2-Step Verification settings.

Can't find it? App Passwords only appear after 2-Step Verification is ON.
4

Create App Password

Select app: Mail
Select device: Other (Custom name) → type Resume Blaster
Click Generate

5

Copy the 16-character password

Google shows a password like abcd efgh ijkl mnop. Click copy. You won't see it again — save it somewhere safe.

6

Paste in Resume Blaster

In the app → Step 4: Configure Sender → paste your Gmail and the App Password.

How to run the app

  1. Download ZIP and unzip
  2. Install Python from python.org (check "Add to PATH")
  3. Double-click start.bat — keep the window open
  4. Open http://localhost:8765 in your browser
  5. Upload resume → write email → add HR emails → click Send to All
Security: Never share your App Password or upload config.json to the internet. Your password stays only on your computer.
