Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 8, 2026

Resume Blaster (https://rushabh023.github.io/resume-blaster/) is a free tool that helps job seekers send their resume to multiple email addresses using their own Gmail account.

What we collect

We do not operate a backend server. We do not store your data on our servers.

  • Resume PDF: Read in your browser only. Sent directly to Gmail via Google's API. Never uploaded to our servers.
  • Email content & recipients: Stay in your browser until sent via your Gmail account.
  • Google Sign-In: We use Google OAuth to send emails on your behalf. We receive a temporary access token — we do not store your Google password.
  • Local storage: Email templates and recipient lists may be saved in your browser's localStorage if you enable "Remember settings."

How we use Google data

Resume Blaster uses the Gmail API scope gmail.send solely to send emails you compose, with your resume attached, to recipients you specify. We do not read your inbox, delete emails, or access any Gmail data beyond sending the emails you request.

Third parties

  • Google Gmail API: Emails are sent through Google's infrastructure using your account.
  • GitHub Pages: Hosts the static website files only (HTML/JS). No user data is processed by GitHub.

Data retention

We do not retain any user data. Tokens expire when you close the browser or sign out. Clear your browser data to remove localStorage settings.

Your responsibilities

You are responsible for complying with anti-spam laws and Gmail's terms of service. Only send emails to recipients where you have a legitimate reason to contact them.
